Chapter One

The Meeting

I never imagined that a single TikTok live would be where it all began. It wasn't just the two of us, our whole barkada was there, hopping on lives together. At first, her camera was off. Then she turned it on, and that's the moment it hit me. It was love at first sight, on a live, of all places.

From then on, we were on TikTok live together almost every single day. It started as simple small talk, but day by day, what I felt for her kept growing bigger. She was in Cagayan back then, far away, but somehow it always felt close whenever we talked.

So we planned a hangout, a laag, in Bawbawon. She was in Cagayan at the time, but she said she'd come all the way home just so she could join us. And she did.

"who knew a live stream could feel like fate."

Chapter Two

The Pursuit

We waited for her at the terminal. When she arrived and I saw her in person for the first time, she was so beautiful, but I couldn't say it, I didn't want to embarrass myself. We went to Bawbawon, we had fun, and the whole time, she was the only one I was looking at, even though I still wasn't showing what I really felt.

The next day, she invited us to a birthday party. When we got there, I saw her in a dress, "oh my god, she is so beautiful." We hung out as a barkada all night, drinks and karaoke, until we finally went home.

That night, I realized my charger was missing, turns out she'd borrowed it to charge the mic for karaoke. The next morning, I messaged her to remind her, and that turned out to be our first ever personal chat, since we usually only talked in our "Le is Che" group chat. I call her "Master" out of respect, since she'd already finished her master's degree.

But she was heading back to Cagayan for work that same day. So I waited for her at the terminal again, to get my charger back, but really, to see her one more time too. She was as beautiful as ever. We only had a few minutes together because it was raining, and even though I wanted to tell her the truth before she left, I still couldn't find the courage. So I just said, "the rain's stopped, I should head home," and left first. A little while later, she got on the bus back to Cagayan.

That night, over chat, that's where I finally told her the truth. Not directly, but I told her that there was someone in our barkada I was in love with. She was curious who it was, until I told her it was her. I asked her: "If I pursue you, would you accept me?" And she answered, "if you're willing to wait."

she gave me a chance. that was all I needed.

Chapter Three

Getting to Know Each Other

To this day, I'm still pursuing Allyn. "If you're willing to wait," that's what she said, and I'm waiting with my whole heart. She's busy with work, sometimes it takes a while before she replies, but no matter how long it takes, I still get excited every time a new message comes in.

She'll be turning 26 this year, and I'll be turning 23, a three year gap between us. But even with that, she still gave me a chance to pursue her, and I don't take that for granted.

This is where we are now, slowly still getting to know each other, in the middle of distance and busy schedules, but I keep loving her and keep waiting, because she's the one I want to reach.

Even the way I say her name has changed along the way, from Allyn, to Llyn, then Ayen, and now, "Do." Small changes, but each one feels like we're getting a little closer.

slowly, but truly. our story is still being written.
